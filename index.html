The history of AI


Past

The 7 original aspects of A.I. (1955):

  1. higher functions of the human brain
  2. Programming a computer to use general language
  3. Arranging Hypothetical neurons in a manner so that they can form concepts (machine learning)
  4. A way to determine and measure problem complexity
  5. Self-improvement (machine learning)
  6. Abstraction: defined as the quality of dealing with ideas rather than events
  7. Randomness and creativity

The official idea and definition of ai was first coined by Jay Mccartney in 1955 at the Dartmouth conference. Of course there was plenty of research work done on ai by others such as Alan Turing before this, but what they were working on was an undefined field before 1955. Quote from John McCarthy at the Dartmouth conference: “Every aspect of learning or any other feature of intelligence can in principle be so exactly described that à machine can be made to simulate it. An attempt will be made to find out how to make machines use language, form abstractions and concepts and solve kinds of problems now reserved for humans and improve themselves.”

In essence ai is a machine with the ability to solve their own problems that are usually done by us humans with our natural intelligence. A computer will demonstrate a form of intelligence when it learns how to improve on themselves at solving these problems.


Present

Currently AI is used all over the world for many different purposes. AI is used to automate a system or improve a systems performance and efficiency. Some stuff AI is currently used for are: Virtual Assistants or chatbots like Siri or Alexa, Agriculture and farming, Autonomous Flying, Retail/shopping, Security and Surveillance, Manufacturing and production, Inventory management, Self-Driving cars/autonomous vehicles, healthcare and much more. Artificial Intelligence (AI) is in essence a machine/program that tries to mimic the problem-solving and decision making capabilities of a human mind. Weak AI is AI trained and focused to perform on specific tasks. Weak AI is most of the current AI that surrounds us today. Weak isn’t the best description for this so it’s also called Artificial Narrow Intelligence (ANI) as it is anything but weak; it enables some very cool applications, such as Siri, Alexa and autonomous vehicles.


Future

Strong AI is made up of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and Artificial Super Intelligence (ASI). Artificial general intelligence (AGI), or general AI, is a theoretical form of AI where a machine would have an intelligence equal to humans it would have some self-awareness and self-consciousness that has the ability to solve problems, learn, and plan for the future. Artificial Super Intelligence (ASI) also known as superintelligence would be better than the intelligence and the ability of the human brain. While strong AI is still entirely theoretical with no practical examples in the use of today, that doesn't mean AI researchers aren't exploring its development.

Machine learning


Machine learning

Machine learning is a part of artificial intelligence (AI) and computer science which focuses on the use of data and algorithms to imitate the way that humans learn, gradually improving its accuracy. In artificial intelligence an expert system is a computer system that is emulating the decision making ability of a human expert. Expert systems are designed to solve complex problems by reasoning through lots and lots of knowledge represented mainly as if then rules rather than through conventional procedural code.


Neural Network & Algorithms

Neural networks can be created from at least three layers of neurons: The input layer, the hidden layers and the output layer. The hidden layer or layers in between have many neurons, with a lot of connections between the layers. As the neural network starts reading the data, the weights and strength of the connections between these neurons are fine tuned allowing the network to come up with accurate predictions. Neural network machine learning algorithms are modeled on the way in which the brain works specifically, the way in which it represents information. When a neural network has a lot of layers it’s called a deep neural network and the process of training and using deep neural networks is called deep learning.
